Art Of Joy - Since 1996

Mayumana is a multicultural troupe, established in 1996 in Tel Aviv.
Its work combines diversity skills: rhythm, dance, music, coordination,
energy and visual effects into a unique humoristic language.
The name of the company comes from the word "Meyumanut" in Hebrew which means "Skill".
Throughout 22 years of creation, the Mayumana group performed in over 90 major cities
around the world, for more than 7 million spectators.





Meet Tal Levy - Cast member since 2012

Tal joind Maumana in 2012 as a perfoemer
In the last 3 years she is also an artistic coordinator and rehearsals manager.
Tal discovered her love for dance and music in a very early age.
She started dancing at the age of 7, practiced many styles over the years,
including Ballet, contemporary, musical theater, hip hop, afro dancehall and more.
Tal is a graduate of "Bikurei Haitim Dance school" and was trained by Naomi Perlov.
In addition, she started play the piano at the age of 7 classical music and years after she also learned Jazz.
She composes music, sings and original pieces written by her were performed by Ramat-Gam chamber orchestra.
Tal says: "music and dance are the most important elememts in my life and I simply can't live without them!"
